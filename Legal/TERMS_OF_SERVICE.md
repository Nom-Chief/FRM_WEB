## 2. Description of the Service

FRM provides tools that help nonprofit fundraisers manage donor relationships and campaigns. Features may include, without limitation:

- **Donor Management:** profiles, tags, relationships, notes, multi-phone, multi-currency, donor merging.
- **Donations & Pledges:** recording one-time and recurring donations, pledge tracking, donation imports/exports.
- **Campaign Tracking:** goals, progress, and reporting.
- **Task & Workflow Management:** Kanban board, reminders, and follow-up scheduling.
- **AI Features:** AI-drafted donor emails, AI-generated call talking points, daily AI-suggested tasks, retrieval-augmented (RAG) chat assistant, and an AI communications modal. AI features are powered by third-party large language model providers (such as Google Generative AI / Gemini and OpenAI-compatible providers).
- **Communication Integrations:** Gmail integration (OAuth-based send/read/sync, history watch, email triage) and SMS via Twilio.
- **Payments:** Stripe-powered donation processing, subscription billing, and billing portal access.
- **Onboarding & Website Builder:** templates and a lightweight website builder for fundraising landing pages.
- **Analytics:** dashboards, charts, donor retention metrics, and aggregated reporting.

We may add, modify, or discontinue features at any time.
