Enterprise-Grade Security for Your Fundraising Data

Your donor and campaign data is protected by industry-standard security measures including Row Level Security (RLS) policies that ensure each organization can only access their own data. All user authentication is handled through secure JWT tokens with phone number verification, ensuring only authorized users can access the system. The platform uses encrypted data transmission and implements organization isolation, meaning your fundraising data is completely separate from other organizations. Additionally, all AI processing is done through secure APIs with no sensitive data stored by external services, giving you complete control over your donor information and campaign details

Meet Navi, Your AI Assistant

Navi helps make your donor relationships smoother, smarter, and more personal. From tracking conversations to surfacing the right insights, Navi helps fundraisers focus on what matters most: building lasting connections.
